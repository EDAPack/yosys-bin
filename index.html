yosys-bin Documentation¶

yosys-bin is a portable, pre-built distribution of the Yosys Open Synthesis Suite for Linux (manylinux 2.34 / glibc 2.34+). It bundles everything needed for RTL synthesis, formal verification preparation, and Python-driven EDA flows in a single self-contained tarball.
