yosys-bin Documentation
=======================

**yosys-bin** is a portable, pre-built distribution of the
`Yosys Open Synthesis Suite <https://yosyshq.net/yosys/>`_ for Linux
(manylinux 2.34 / glibc 2.34+). It bundles everything needed for RTL
synthesis, formal verification preparation, and Python-driven EDA flows
in a single self-contained tarball.
